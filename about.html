Our Story

Wasabi15 is a modern Asian cuisine restaurant located in the heart of Viman Nagar, Pune. We bring together authentic Asian flavors with a contemporary dining experience.

From handcrafted sushi to flavorful Pan-Asian dishes, every plate is prepared with passion, premium ingredients, and artistic presentation.

Whether it’s a casual dinner, celebration, or date night, Wasabi15 offers an unforgettable culinary journey.
